Lolgi Action Ring is a Windows desktop application that lets you configure
and trigger shortcut, application, file, folder, URL, and system actions.
This policy explains how [Publisher legal name] ("we", "us", or "our")
handles information when you use the application.

SUMMARY

Lolgi Action Ring is local-first. It does not require an account, does not
include advertising, and does not send analytics, usage telemetry, or your
action configuration to us. Most information stays on your Windows device.

The app makes network requests only when you choose a feature that needs an
external service, such as searching Iconify for an icon or obtaining a favicon
for a URL action. Those requests are described below.

INFORMATION STORED ON YOUR DEVICE

Lolgi Action Ring stores its configuration in its local Windows application
data folder. Depending on what you configure, this can include:

- Ring profiles, hotkey, visual settings, and enabled actions.
- Labels, icons, shortcuts, macro text, command text, URLs, and file or folder
  paths you assign to actions.
- Application-profile information such as a detected application's process name
  and executable path.

This information is used only to provide the app's configured functionality.
It is not automatically uploaded to us.

DIAGNOSTICS

The app retains up to 100 recent diagnostic events locally to help explain
action and ring failures. These may include timestamps, action type and result,
profile name or ID, process name, executable filename (without its directory),
application/window state, and build information. The diagnostic log is stored locally in the app's
application-data folder.

The app does not upload diagnostic events. If you choose Copy last diagnostic,
the selected diagnostic information is copied to your Windows clipboard so that
you can decide whether to share it with support or another person.

OPERATING-SYSTEM ACCESS

To provide its features, the app may access or use Windows functionality to:

- Detect the foreground application and its executable path to select a ring
  profile.
- Read local application, file, or folder icons that you choose to use.
- Open a file, folder, application, command, or URL when you trigger an action.
- Send the keyboard shortcut or sequence you configured to the target
  application.
- Copy a diagnostic report to the clipboard when you explicitly request it.

The app does not read the contents of your clipboard. A configured paste action
sends the normal paste shortcut to the target application; it does not inspect
the clipboard contents.

OPTIONAL THIRD-PARTY NETWORK REQUESTS

The following requests occur only when you use the related feature:

- Icon search or icon download: Iconify API (api.iconify.design). Your icon
  search terms and requested icon identifiers are sent, together with normal
  connection information such as your IP address.
- URL-action favicon lookup: Google Favicon service, with DuckDuckGo Icons as
  a fallback. The hostname of the URL you entered is sent, together with normal
  connection information such as your IP address.
- Opening a URL action: Your selected browser and the destination website
  receive the URL you configured. The browser and destination site's own
  privacy policies apply.

These providers process requests under their own privacy policies. We do not
receive the information sent to them merely because you use these features.
You can avoid the icon and favicon requests by not using those optional
features.

SHARING AND SALE OF INFORMATION

We do not sell personal information. We do not operate an app backend that
receives your configuration, diagnostic logs, action history, or foreground-app
information. We share information only when you direct the app to use an
external service or open a destination you configured, as described above.

RETENTION AND DELETION

Your configuration and local diagnostics remain on your device until you change
or delete them, or remove the app's local data. Uninstalling the app may not
remove that local data automatically. Diagnostic retention is capped at 100
events, with newer events replacing older events.

SECURITY

We design the app to keep configuration and diagnostics local. No security
method is perfect, so protect your Windows account and device, especially if
your action configuration contains sensitive paths, commands, or URLs.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Lolgi Action Ring is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect
personal information from children.
